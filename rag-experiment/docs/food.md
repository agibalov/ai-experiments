# pizza

Here’s a straightforward homemade pizza recipe:

---

## Ingredients (1 large pizza)

* 2 cups (250 g) all-purpose flour
* ¾ cup (180 ml) warm water
* 2 tsp olive oil
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 tsp dry yeast
* ½ cup pizza sauce
* 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
* Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, etc.)

---

## Steps

1. **Make the dough**

   * Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5 minutes until foamy.
   * Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead 5–7 minutes until smooth.
   * Cover and let rise about 1 hour, until doubled.

2. **Prepare the oven**

   * Preheat oven to 475 °F (245 °C).

3. **Shape the crust**

   * Roll dough into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
   * Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

4. **Assemble**

   * Spread sauce evenly.
   * Sprinkle cheese.
   * Add toppings.

5. **Bake**

   * Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

6. **Finish**

   * Remove, let cool slightly, then slice and serve.

# chicken soup

Here’s a simple chicken soup recipe:

---

## Ingredients

* 1 whole chicken (about 3–4 lb) or 2–3 lb chicken parts (with bones)
* 10 cups water or chicken broth
* 2 carrots, sliced
* 2 celery stalks, sliced
* 1 onion, quartered
* 2 garlic cloves (optional)
* 1–2 bay leaves
* Salt and pepper to taste
* Fresh parsley or dill for garnish
* (Optional: noodles, rice, or potatoes)

---

## Steps

1. **Prepare chicken**

   * Rinse and trim excess fat.

2. **Simmer broth**

   * Place chicken in a large pot with water or broth, onion, garlic, and bay leaves.
   * Bring to a boil, skim foam, then reduce to low simmer for 45–60 minutes.

3. **Add vegetables**

   * Add carrots and celery. Simmer another 20–30 minutes until chicken is fully cooked and vegetables tender.

4. **Optional starch**

   * Add noodles, rice, or diced potatoes during the last 10–15 minutes of cooking.

5. **Finish**

   * Remove chicken, shred meat, discard bones and skin, return meat to the pot.
   * Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.

6. **Serve**

   * Ladle into bowls and garnish with parsley or dill.

# tuna sandwich

Here’s a simple tuna sandwich recipe:

---

## Ingredients

* 1 can tuna (5–6 oz), drained
* 2 slices bread (white, wheat, or rye)
* 2 tbsp mayonnaise
* 1 tsp mustard (optional)
* 1 celery stalk, finely chopped (optional)
* 1 tbsp onion, finely chopped (optional)
* Lettuce or tomato slices (optional)
* Salt and pepper to taste

---

## Steps

1. **Prepare filling**

   * In a bowl, mix drained tuna with mayonnaise.
   * Stir in mustard, celery, onion, salt, and pepper if using.

2. **Assemble sandwich**

   * Spread tuna mixture evenly onto one slice of bread.
   * Add lettuce or tomato if desired.
   * Place the second slice of bread on top.

3. **Serve**

   * Cut in half and serve immediately.

# syrniki

Here’s a traditional **syrniki** (Russian/Ukrainian cottage cheese pancakes) recipe:

---

## Ingredients

* 500 g farmer’s cheese (tvorog, well-drained)
* 1 large egg
* 2–3 tbsp sugar
* 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
* Pinch of salt
* 3–4 tbsp flour (plus extra for dusting)
* Oil or butter for frying

---

## Steps

1. **Prepare cheese mixture**

   * Mash farmer’s cheese with a fork until smooth.
   * Add egg, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Mix well.
   * Stir in flour until the dough holds together but is still soft.

2. **Shape syrniki**

   * Lightly flour your hands and surface.
   * Divide dough into small balls (about golf ball size).
   * Flatten gently into thick patties. Dust with flour.

3. **Fry**

   * Heat a nonstick pan with a little oil or butter over medium heat.
   * Fry syrniki 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.

4. **Serve**

   * Serve warm with sour cream, jam, honey, or fresh berries.
